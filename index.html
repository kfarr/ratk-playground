Reality Accelerator Toolkit Demo

  • Visualizing Tracked Planes and Meshes: Displays wireframe outlines of real-world planes and meshes.
  • Interactive Hit-Testing: Allows users to conduct hit tests against physical space.
  • Persistent Anchors Creation: Enables users to create persistent markers in the MR space, shown as green blocks.
